I know I'm destined for greatness, even if my current situation doesn't look like it. I'm impulsive and proud, which is probably how a mischief-maker like me ended up in a bridge crew.

I'm a thief by trade—nimble, fast-talking, and good at getting out of trouble. My main goal right now is to earn my freedom or find a way to escape this crew, but I do feel a certain loyalty to the others in Bridge Nine, specifically the kid Aran and the old man Doral.

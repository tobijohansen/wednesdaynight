Radiant Orders

Dustbringers: Trait: (Self-Mastery)You believe that great power requires even greater self-control. You may have a tinkerer’s mind, enjoying taking things apart to see how they work. You understand that you have the potential for great destruction, so you prioritize discipline and self-mastery to ensure you don't harm the wrong people.

Playstyle:You want to use destructive power (splitting molecular bonds or increasing friction), but you approach it with precision rather than wild abandon → Surges: Abrasion, Division → Class: Warrior or Agent
They wrestle with destructive power and self-control. Warriors capture the raw might, Agents bring the precision and restraint side.
Edgedancers (Remembrance) → Envoy or Scholar
They serve those forgotten by society. An Envoy's empathy and a Scholar's healing or medicine skills both fit beautifully.
Elsecallers (Potential) → Scholar
Elsecallers pursue knowledge and transformation. The Scholar's emphasis on lore, intellect, and crafting flows right into their ideals.
Lightweavers (Secrecy) → Envoy or Agent
Lightweavers excel in persuasion, artistry, and deception. Envoy reflects the charm side, Agent the infiltration side.
Skybreakers (Justice) → Agent
Skybreakers enforce the law through investigation and punishment. The Agent's stealth, insight, and discipline match their "judge and executioner" vibe.
Stonewards (Tenacity) → Warrior or Leader
The tireless soldiers of Roshar. Warriors embody their endurance, Leaders their reliability as anchors for groups.
Truthwatchers (Truth) → Scholar
They seek and share knowledge, with a touch of mystical insight. The Scholar's lore-driven talents sync well with their investigative spirit.
Willshapers (Freedom) → Hunter or Envoy
They value independence and exploration. Hunter emphasizes survival and scouting, Envoy emphasizes inspiring freedom in others.
Windrunners (Protection)You are driven by a desire to defend those who cannot defend themselves. You likely value martial excellence and thrive within a team or military structure.

Playstyle: You want to be in the thick of combat, using gravity to fly or walk on walls to reach those in danger. → Warrior or Leader
They're all about defending others, holding the line, and inspiring troops. Strength and Presence align with their oaths.
